The Artist

About

Charly Ponziani grew up between Italy and Northern Ireland, moved through France, England, Spain and eventually Belfast — and it was there, far from where she started, that she began to compose.

Pianist, vocalist and maker of intimate, cinematic music. Her compositions begin with emotion and end there too — every note chosen not just to sound beautiful, but to feel like something. Something personal, something intentional, something that stays. Music had always lived in her family; Belfast was where it became her own. Her composition Aurelia was recently featured on BBC Radio Introducing. Bilingual in Italian and English, fluent in Spanish, she is now based in Milan.
